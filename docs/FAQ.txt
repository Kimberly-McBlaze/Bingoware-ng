Bingoware-ng: Frequently Asked Questions

This FAQ covers Bingoware-ng, a bingo system for visualization and some gameplay.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Q: What is Bingoware-ng?
A: Bingoware-ng is a bingo system originally created as Bingoware. It provides visualization and some gameplay.

Q: Where can I get the latest version?
A: Bingoware-ng is maintained on GitHub. Historical releases and references may still exist on SourceForge:
   https://sourceforge.net/projects/bingoware/

Q: Why do some pages or documentation still mention "Bingoware"?
A: Some references are kept for historical attribution (e.g., "Based on Bingoware v1.5...") or to preserve original URLs.
   User-facing branding should refer to Bingoware-ng.

Q: Can I use old Bingoware boards/levels with Bingoware-ng?
A: In most cases, yes. Compatibility is generally maintained, but some features/settings may differ.

Q: Is Bingoware-ng free software?
A: Yes. Bingoware-ng is distributed under the GNU GPL v3 (or later).
